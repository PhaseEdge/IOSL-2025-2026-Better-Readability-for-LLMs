All About the Platypus

The platypus is a unique mammal native to eastern Australia. It is one of the few mammals that lay eggs.

Platypus Facts

  • Scientific Name: Ornithorhynchus anatinus
  • Habitat: Freshwater rivers and lakes in Australia
  • Diet: Insects, larvae, shellfish, and worms
  • Unique Feature: It has a duck-like bill and venomous spurs on males
