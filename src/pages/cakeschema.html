Classic Chocolate Cake Recipe

A simple, moist chocolate cake for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 100g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250ml buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a 9-inch round pan.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, mix oil, eggs, and vanilla. Gradually add buttermilk.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into pan. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Pro Tip

For extra richness, add 100g dark chocolate chips to the batter.
